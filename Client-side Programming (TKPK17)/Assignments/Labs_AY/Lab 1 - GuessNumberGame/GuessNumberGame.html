Guess the number game

Description

Simple guess the number type game. Application choose a random number between 1 and 100, then challenge the player to guess the number in 5 turns. After each turn the player can check in CONSOLE if his answer is right or wrong, and if they are wrong, whether the guess was too low or too high.
